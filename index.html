I am a second year Ph.D student in Computer Science at the Illinois Institute of Technology, under the supervision of Professor Binghui Wang. My research addresses critical security and safety challenges within the domain of multimodal AI. Specifically, I investigate the vulnerabilities of large multimodal models by designing and evaluating novel backdoor attacks against Vision Language Models (VLMs) and Text-to-Image diffusion models (TTI). The objective of my work is to characterize these emergent threats to inform the development of robust countermeasures and establish a foundation for trustworthy multimodal systems.
